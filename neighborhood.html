the neighborhood

I live in orem by state street and center street. Most people live in apartment complexes and we're surrounded by different businesses. Here are some places of interest and local restaurants:

  • Orem Public Library
  • Orem City Park
  • Orem Skate Park
  • Imax Movie Theater
  • Lomito's Restaurant
  • Joe's Cafe
  • Kohinoor Restaurant
  • Asa Ramen

I like living in this neighborhood because everything you need is nearby. There are also a lot of good local restaurants and the movie theater is close. It's not very family friendly, but who cares, I don't have a family.
